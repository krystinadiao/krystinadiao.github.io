Korean Dance Crew


I was introducted to Korean Dance Crew in 2015, as they were going through a rebranding. Since then, I have helped them with promotional videos, live performance recordings, logo design, rebranding, and website development.

Throughout this project process, I have been given the opportunity to work practice my design and development skills, while working with my first "client" type project.

This project is still under development.

Research / Analysis

The first step in tackling this project was identifying the user personas who will interact with any KDC material (web, social, promotional) and what specific use cases may entail.

The landing page mockup is designed with the purpose of showcasing KDC's most recent promotional video on a 20 second full-screen video background loop. A simple horizontal navigation is highlighted by red lower border on hover.

The member page mock up shows an uncluttered view of the member profiles. Things that I plan to implement and challenge myself on include unique UI that will engage users, easy to use UX and simple information architecture, and useful implementations such as Google Calendar and upcoming events.
