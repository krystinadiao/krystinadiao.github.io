Strategy / Background

Korean Dance Crew is an on-campus, student-run dance crew. I was introducted to KDC in 2015, as they were going through a rebranding. Previously known as "KUSA Dance Crew", KDC was now independent of KUSA and required all new branding material to reflect "Korean Dance Crew". Since then, I have helped them with promotional videos, live performance recordings, logo design, rebranding, and website development.

Throughout this project process, I have been given the opportunity to work practice my design and development skills, while working with my first "client" type project.

With constant communication with the KDC head choreographers and project development check-ins, I have developed my project management skills.

This project is still under development.
