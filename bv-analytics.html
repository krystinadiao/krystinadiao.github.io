Bazaarvoice Analytics


This project was my primary project at Bazaarvoice during the summer of 2016. My task was to analyze the effectiveness of a newly implemented knowledge base. The product at hand, Connections, had previously no knowledge base and very brief documentation. Thus, the purpose of the project was to quantitatively depict the necessity of product knowledge documentation and it's direct effect on support.

Problem

Proving the quantitative value of product knowledge material on an existing product. What is the impact on support, clients, engineering, and the company overall?

Approach

This project presented itself as a more research based project. I wanted to grasp the opportunity at a large software company to submerge myself in more development-based projects, thus I created a developmental edge for myself. I decided to create the end analytics with JavaScript data visualizations, rather than mundane pivot tables or other graphing tools.

With little to no prior knowledge in JavaScript, I learned many things on the job, along with the assistance and mentorship of those around me. I ultimately created my visualizations with D3 and C3 libraries. I gathered the data from Salesforce, analyzed the numbers, identified the KPIs that I would need to be tracking over the course of a couple months, and created the final results with actionable insights.

You can view, and read more in depth about, the project here: Executive Summary

Conclusion

The final project was very well received, being taken to C-level meetings to demonstrate the necessity of product knowledge content.
