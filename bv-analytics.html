My involvement with Korean Dance Crew, an on-campus, student-run dance crew, runs on various levels. I have helped them with promotional videos, live performance recordings, logo design, rebranding, and website development.

Throughout this project process, I have been given the opportunity to work practice my design and development skills, while working with my first "client" type project.

With constant communication with the KDC head choreographers and weekly project development check-ins, I have developed my professional communication skills.

This project is still under development.
Photoshop, Illustrator
Along with their 2015 rebranding of the dance crew, KDC also requested a logo redesign. Displayed above are the logos I came up with given the requirements. The purpose of the rebranding was primarily to reflect the shift in name from "KUSA Dance Crew" to "Korean Dance Crew". This faced us with the challenge of maintaining the sleek, hip, street style of the crew, while upping the modernity and design flow.

I created a set of about 5 variations on Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator, and had the KDC members vote on their favorite. We concluded on the most upper left design, but with the removal of the date.
